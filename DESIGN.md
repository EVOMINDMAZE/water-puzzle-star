## Water Puzzle

Classic water-decanting logic puzzle styled as a hyper-casual mobile playable ad.

**Core mechanic:** Tap a source bottle → tap a destination bottle to pour until one is full or empty. Reach the exact target volume to win.

**Art style:** Flat minimalist — thick black outlines, vibrant orange liquid, red dashed target line, proportional bottle heights, bold fraction labels. Dark bottom banner with Undo/Restart/Moves/CTA.
